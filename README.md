# PrimeForm

PrimeForm is a calm, intelligent fitness application focused on **clarity, consistency, and long-term habit building**.

It combines structured fitness principles with AI-assisted coaching to help users train, eat, and progress without confusion, overwhelm, or obsession.

PrimeForm is designed especially for:
- beginners who feel lost
- people restarting fitness later in life
- busy adults who want guidance without micromanagement

---

## Product Philosophy

**What PrimeForm believes**
- Consistency matters more than precision
- Habits come before optimisation
- Calm guidance beats aggressive motivation
- Trends matter more than daily noise
- AI should guide and encourage — not judge or shame

**What PrimeForm is not**
- Not a calorie database race
- Not a social fitness platform
- Not a “30-day transformation” app
- Not an analytics overload tool

---

## Core Features (Current & Planned)

### Training
- Personalized workout plans based on:
  - training level
  - equipment access (gym / home / dumbbells / bodyweight)
  - days per week
- Workout session tracking
- Progressive expansion toward:
  - custom exercises
  - multiple tracking modes (e.g. weight+reps, time+reps, distance+time)

---

### Nutrition
- AI-generated daily calorie targets
- Protein-focused guidance (macros simplified by default)
- Simple food logging (no forced micromanagement)
- Weekly trend-based adjustments (planned)
- Local food support and barcode scanning (planned)

---

### AI Coaching
- Plain-language explanations of plans
- Conservative, safety-first guidance
- Trend interpretation (when to stay the course vs adjust)
- Encouragement and consistency feedback

---

### Identity & Motivation (Planned)
- Aspiration archetypes:
  - celebrity or fictional character “paths”
  - framed as realistic trajectories, not comparisons
- Optional progress photo check-ins with:
  - encouraging, coach-style feedback
  - no ratings or harsh critique
  - privacy-first handling

---

## Monetisation

PrimeForm uses a **free + premium** model.

- Core habit-building features remain free
- Advanced customisation, automation, and insights are gated
- Subscriptions and entitlements are handled via RevenueCat
- Paywall behaviour can be remotely toggled (OFF / SOFT / HARD)

---

## Long-Term Vision

PrimeForm aims to become:
> A trusted, calm fitness companion people rely on — like MyFitnessPal for logging, but more humane, personalised, and intelligent.

The app grows with the user:
- simple when they need structure
- powerful when they’re ready for control
- always grounded in realism and safety
